Your 5-Year Roadmap

The path from paycheck-to-paycheck to financial freedom.

Year 1
Build the Foundation
Create your budget, build a $1,000 emergency fund, pay off high-interest debt. Automate your savings. Start investing even $50/month.
Year 2
Accelerate Income
Negotiate a raise or find a higher-paying job. Start a side hustle. Increase savings rate to 20-30%. Build 3-6 months emergency fund.
Milestone

The hardest milestone. After this, compound interest does the heavy lifting. Your money starts making money.

Year 5+
Coast to Freedom

The Playbook

Wealth-Building Strategies

Proven tactics our members use to accelerate their path to financial independence.

Pay Yourself First

Automate transfers to savings and investments the day you get paid. What's left is what you spend — not the other way around.

Index Fund Investing

Stop trying to pick stocks. Low-cost index funds beat 90% of active managers over time. Set it and forget it.

House Hacking

Buy a multi-unit property, live in one unit, rent the others. Let tenants pay your mortgage while you build equity.

Time in Market > Timing

Start now. Don't wait for the "perfect" moment. A dollar invested today is worth more than a dollar invested tomorrow.

Tax-Advantaged Accounts

Max out your 401k, IRA, and HSA before investing in taxable accounts. Free money from employer matches. Tax-free growth.

Skill Stacking

Combine 2-3 in-demand skills to become rare and valuable. Rare skills = higher income = faster path to freedom.
